About

SuSOcean is a Korea–Norway collaborative research project focused on smart and sustainable ocean structures.

The project integrates international collaboration, advanced materials, and marine infrastructure research.

Duration: 2025–2028
Countries: Korea, Norway

Project Objectives

The SuSOcean project aims to develop sustainable ocean infrastructure through advanced materials, smart monitoring systems, and international collaboration.

Research Scope

  • Smart ocean structures
  • Sustainable composite materials
  • Structural health monitoring systems

International Collaboration

This project involves collaboration between Korea and Norway, including SeoulTech, Seoul National University, Oslo Metropolitan University, and the University of Agder.

Expected Outcomes

The project aims to contribute to sustainable marine infrastructure, global research collaboration, and advanced engineering education.
