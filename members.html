Professor

Min Ook Kim

Seoul National University of Science and Technology

Ph.D. in Civil Engineering. Professor Kim specializes in eco-friendly composite materials and structural durability.

His research focuses on fiber-reinforced polymer (FRP), sustainable construction materials, and long-term performance of concrete structures in marine environments.

He leads the Eco-friendly Composite Materials Lab and actively participates in international collaborative research including the SuSOcean project.
